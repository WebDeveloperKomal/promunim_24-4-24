Aadhar Verification offline

•   Lets verify your identity & address via Digilocker service a secure platform provided by Govt of India.

•   All we need you to do is complete 4 simple steps.

     1. Enter your Aadhar

     2. Enter OTP Received in your Aadhar linked mobile number.

     3. Enter the 6 digit M-PIN of Digilocker. To reset M-PIN Click here.

     4. Give consent to fetch your details.

•   I provide my consent for the. Following

 1. Visit UIDAI (Aadhar) website here.

 2. Login with Aadhar and OTP in registered mobile number in your Aadhar.

 3. Select offline e-KYC from service list shown.

 4. create a 4 digit share code.

 5. download the ZIP file.

 6. Upload the file in our next screen along with the created 4 digit share code.
